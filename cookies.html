Short version: we don't use marketing cookies, advertising trackers, or behavioural profiling. The only cookies set by this site are strictly necessary for the site to function. There's nothing to opt out of, but we list them anyway so you can verify.

01What's a cookie.

A cookie is a small piece of data stored in your browser by websites you visit. It's used to remember things between page loads — like a session, a preference, or whether you've dismissed a banner. Cookies can be strictly necessary, functional, analytics, or marketing; we use only the first.

02What we use.

This website is intentionally minimal. We use:

  • One session cookie for form submissions, to prevent double-submission and basic CSRF protection
  • One preference cookie, set only if you submit the newsletter form, to remember that you've subscribed
  • No analytics cookies — page views are counted via a self-hosted Plausible instance that does not set cookies and does not identify users
  • No advertising cookies — we don't run ads or work with ad networks

04Third parties.

We embed two assets from third parties — Google Fonts for typefaces and jsDelivr for the GSAP animation library. These services may log request metadata (IP address, user-agent) but do not set cookies on this domain.

For clients with strict no-third-party policies, both are easy to self-host — write to privacy@xrd.technologies and we can document the swap.

05Your choices.

You can clear cookies for this site any time from your browser settings. Doing so will not affect site functionality except that the newsletter "subscribed" state will be forgotten. Browser-level "Do Not Track" signals are honoured.

06Contact.

Questions about cookies, tracking, or third-party assets — email privacy@xrd.technologies. Our security & privacy team responds within five working days.
