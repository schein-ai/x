Open studio · 1st Friday, May.

Drop by the Da Nang HQ — drawings on the walls, prototypes on the tables, beer in the courtyard. 18:00–21:00, no appointment needed.

xR&D Studio Da Nang HQ · Monthly
10 Apr 2026
Issue 005

Once a month, on the first Friday, we open the studio to whoever wants to drop by. No appointment, no pitch, no programme. Drawings on the walls, prototypes on the tables, the team around to talk to. The next one is Friday 02 May, 18:00 to 21:00, at the Da Nang HQ.

01 What it isAn open studio, not an event.

The closest thing this resembles is an architecture firm's open studio night. Walk in. Wander. Look at what's on the walls. Pick up a prototype. Ask questions. Stay as long as you want to. Leave when the beer runs out, which it will around 21:00.

It is deliberately unprogrammed. There is no opening keynote, no panel, no pitch deck. The team will be working — actually working, on actual engagements — and we'll talk to anyone who's curious. The goal is to lower the friction of meeting us by an order of magnitude.

02 Who comesMostly curious people.

Past attendees, in rough proportion: clients dropping by between meetings (lots), founders of other Da Nang studios (some), university students from the partner programme (always), travelling friends-of-friends (a few), people who just walked past and saw the lights on (occasionally — these are usually the best conversations).

  • Clients welcome — current and prospective
  • Engineers, designers, founders welcome
  • Students welcome (especially)
  • Friends of friends welcome
  • Just walking past welcome

03 What you'll seeLive work.

The walls have whatever the studio is mid-work on — prompt boards, system diagrams, visual roughs from current campaigns, the running notes from internal reviews. Most of it stays up between months. New things get added the morning of each open studio.

If something on the wall is sensitive (client work that's not yet shipped), we'll cover it for the night. About 80% of what we're doing is showable.

04 The formatNo schedule.

There is no schedule. Some things happen reliably:

  • Around 18:30 — most of the team is around, beer is cold, music is on
  • Around 19:00 — informal demo of one current build, if someone asks
  • Around 20:00 — the courtyard fills up, conversations get longer
  • Around 21:00 — wind-down, cleanup, last drinks

If you want a tour, ask. If you want to be left alone to look around, do that. Both are fine.

05 Getting thereThe address.

The HQ is in central Da Nang, ten minutes by Grab from the riverside. The full address ships in the Friday newsletter the morning of. If you're in town and don't get the newsletter, email hello@xrd.studio by Thursday and we'll send it across.

For visitors flying in: Da Nang International is a 20-minute taxi from the studio. If you tell us in advance, we'll plan dinner before or breakfast the next morning depending on your schedule.

06 What's afterOther Fridays.

The first Friday of every month, all year, until further notice. The next dates are 02 May, 06 June, 04 July, 01 August. We'll skip nothing unless something exceptional comes up; if we do skip, we'll publish a note on Insights ahead of time.

If you can't make it but want to come another time, that's fine too — we welcome visitors most weekdays by appointment. Email and we'll find a slot.
