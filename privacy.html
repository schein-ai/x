This policy explains what data we collect when you use xR&D's website, services, and tools, why we collect it, and the choices you have. We try to keep it short and plain — if anything is unclear, write to privacy@xrd.technologies.

01Who we are.

xR&D Technologies is an AI-native company headquartered in Da Nang, Vietnam, with a sub-office in Hanoi. For the purposes of GDPR, the data controller is xR&D Technologies Co., Ltd., Da Nang, Vietnam.

02What we collect.

We collect only what we need to run the studio and serve our clients:

  • Contact details — name, email, organisation, phone — when you submit our contact, intake, or newsletter forms
  • Project briefs — the information you choose to share when enquiring about a project
  • Aggregate analytics — anonymised page views via a self-hosted Plausible instance, no cookies, no cross-site tracking
  • Server logs — request URLs, response codes, and timestamps, retained for 30 days for security review

We do not use third-party advertising trackers, behavioural profiling, or session-replay tools.

03How we use it.

Information is used solely to respond to your enquiry, deliver services you've engaged us for, send the newsletter you subscribed to, and operate the website securely. We do not sell, rent, or share data for marketing purposes.

04Sharing & processors.

The following processors may handle limited personal data on our behalf:

  • Hetzner Cloud (Germany) — primary hosting, EU-located
  • Postmark (USA) — transactional email; standard contractual clauses in place
  • Buttondown (USA) — newsletter delivery; opt-in only

Where data leaves the EU, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses and additional encryption at rest and in transit. We never share data with public authorities except when legally compelled.

05Your rights.

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you. You may also withdraw consent for the newsletter at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email. We respond to all requests within 30 days.

06Retention.

Enquiry data is retained for 24 months unless we enter into an engagement, in which case retention follows the contract. Newsletter data is retained until you unsubscribe. Server logs are kept for 30 days. Anonymised analytics are aggregated indefinitely but cannot be linked back to individuals.

07Contact.

For privacy questions, data requests, or complaints, please write to privacy@xrd.technologies. EU residents may also contact our representative at eu-rep@xrd.technologies or lodge a complaint with their local supervisory authority.
