FAQ

Common questions.

The questions principals ask in the first conversation. Don't see yours? Email a partner.

    Will AI replace our engineers?

    No, and we wouldn't sell it as one. The AI-driven build process means our engineers ship faster — more time on architecture, integration, and edge cases, less time on boilerplate. Clients who run a project with us typically spend less in total than they would on a slower in-house build, with comparable or better quality.

    What happens to our IP and our codebase?

    It stays yours. We deliver into your repositories from day one — your code, your branches, your reviews. Engagement-specific data and prompts stay in your tenant. We don't accumulate client IP on our side, and nothing is used to train external models.

    Security review with your team is included in every Project engagement.

    What infrastructure do we need?

    We're cloud-flexible — AWS, GCP, or Azure all fine. We can also deploy on-prem for clients with sovereignty requirements. Model providers: typically OpenAI, Anthropic, or open-weight (we'll discuss the trade-offs upfront), all hosted in your tenant.

    Can we start without a long commitment?

    Yes — that's the POC tier. A 4–12 week proof of concept on a real problem. Most clients use the POC to validate fit before scoping a longer Project engagement; about most convert to a Project afterward, but there's no obligation, and you keep all output regardless.

    How does this fit with the other businesses?

    AI-Driven App Development (this business) builds software. AI Visual Production generates the visuals around it. AI Adoption Consulting sets the strategy and then flows into our build. AI-Native Talent places engineers into your team for internalisation. They're separate scopes, often combined.
