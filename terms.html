These terms govern use of the xR&D website and any services delivered under a separate engagement letter. Where a signed engagement contradicts these terms, the engagement governs.

01Scope.

By accessing this website you agree to these terms. The website is provided for informational purposes — it does not constitute a contract for services. Engagements are formed only via a separate written and signed agreement (an engagement letter or master services agreement).

02Engagements.

All client work is delivered under a written engagement that defines scope, deliverables, fees, schedule, and any specific terms. Standard engagement tiers (Pilot, Studio Partnership, Enterprise) carry default terms which are tailored at signing.

  • Acceptance criteria are defined per deliverable in the engagement letter
  • Change requests outside of agreed scope are billed separately at the prevailing studio rate
  • Confidentiality applies to all engagement materials by default; mutual NDA available on request

03IP & ownership.

Upon full payment, client receives a non-exclusive, perpetual licence to deliverables for the agreed project. xR&D retains ownership of underlying methodology, libraries, model weights, and pre-existing tools, and may reuse non-confidential design knowledge in subsequent work.

For Enterprise tier, tuned model weights are transferred in full as part of the engagement, subject to a separate model-use licence.

04Fees & payment.

Fees are quoted in USD net of taxes. Standard payment terms are 50% on engagement signing, 50% on milestone delivery, with payment due within 14 days of invoice. Late payments accrue interest at 1.5% per month or the legal maximum, whichever is lower.

05Warranty & liability.

We warrant that services will be performed with the skill and care expected of a professional architectural studio. We do not warrant outcomes (planning approvals, regulatory consents, or third-party performance). To the fullest extent permitted by law, our aggregate liability is capped at fees paid for the engagement giving rise to the claim.

Generative outputs are candidate schemes for review and adaptation — not stamped designs. All deliverables require professional review and, where applicable, registered architect sign-off in the project's jurisdiction.

06Term & termination.

Either party may terminate an engagement for material breach with 30 days' written notice and an opportunity to cure. On termination, fees accrue pro-rata for work delivered up to the termination date, and licences for delivered work survive subject to payment.

07Disputes.

These terms are governed by the laws of Vietnam. Disputes are first addressed by good-faith negotiation, then by mediation, then by binding arbitration in Đà Nẵng under VIAC rules. The prevailing party may recover reasonable legal fees.
