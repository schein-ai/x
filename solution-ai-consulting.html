FAQ

Common questions.

The questions executives ask in the first conversation. Don't see yours? Email a partner.

    How is this different from a Big 4 / strategy consultancy?

    Three differences. One: we run an AI-native company ourselves, on the same playbook we sell. Two: our consulting flows directly into our engineering and talent teams — no vendor handoff, no deck-to-build translation losses. Three: we're light. A 4–8 week assessment gives you a roadmap, not 200 pages of research.

    If you want a slow, brand-name engagement with no implementation, you'll be happier elsewhere.

    Will the assessment lead to vendor lock-in?

    No. The assessment is yours, fully documented, and you can take it to any implementation partner — including in-house. Most clients choose to continue with us into build because it's faster, but there's no contractual obligation. Roughly two-thirds continue, but the third who don't keep all output regardless.

    Can you handle ASEAN-expanded operations?

    Yes — Japanese companies with ASEAN operations are a sub-target for us. Our Da Nang HQ is well-placed for ASEAN-region rollouts, and our team is Japanese-language native. Multi-site engagements typically include site visits and local-language workstreams.

    What about Claude Code / development tool adoption?

    Yes — Claude Code adoption is a specific sub-engagement we run. Tool selection, internal guidelines, hands-on training, operational rollout. We use Claude Code daily ourselves, so the operational knowledge is real, not theoretical.

    How does this fit with the other businesses?

    AI Adoption Consulting (this business) sets the strategy and designs the system. AI-Driven App Development builds it. AI Visual Production generates the visual artefacts the rollout needs. AI-Native Talent places engineers into your team for internalisation. Same team, four entry points.
