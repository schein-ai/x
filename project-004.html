The premise

SKU × language × channel — at the cadence the channels set.

SNS algorithms reward fresh short-form content. Brands that miss the cadence lose reach; brands that try to hit it with traditional production lose their margin. The math isn't friendly for either side.

We turned the math around. Each product, each language, each channel-format — generated from the same master concept and the same brand guides. Short-form video at the volume the channels actually need, without renting a shoot for every SKU.

Master files are kept by the client. The pipeline absorbs new products as the catalogue grows.

The approach

Four moves, one channel-ready batch.

From product data and brand guides, the pipeline generates and adapts at SKU-scale. Each move sets up the next batch to be cheaper.

Move 01

Product data

SKU catalogue, key visuals, product features, brand voice — encoded into the generation pipeline once.

Move 02

Master concepts

A small set of master concepts approved by brand. Every video derives from one of them; no creative lift per SKU.

Move 03

Generate at scale

SKU × language × channel-format combinations. 50+ videos out of one set of inputs. Multi-format outputs in parallel.

Move 04

Distribute & iterate

Performance feedback into the next batch. Concepts that win get more variations; concepts that don't get retired.
