A small studio,
built to last.

We're 23 people across two Vietnam offices, and we plan to grow slowly. We hire carefully, pay fairly, and try to keep the work interesting for everyone — founders and interns alike.

If you build, design, consult, train, or think with AI for a living, and you're curious about what an AI-native company can be — we'd like to meet you. The list below is what's open today; speculative letters are also welcome.

i.

Not just users of AI — we build with it. Code, visuals, research: every part runs through AI as a default tool.

ii.

iii.

JP × VN collaboration.

Two Japanese founders, Vietnamese staff. We respect both cultures while delivering for both contexts.

We write up what we learn. Insights gets fed by everyone — what worked, what didn't, both go out.

Benefits · The basics

What we offer, plainly.

01 · Pay
Transparent bands.

Salary bands are published internally. The same role earns the same anywhere; cost-of-living is added on top.

02 · Time
30 days, no questions.

Annual leave plus public holidays. Sabbatical month every five years. We expect you to take it.

03 · Health
Full coverage.

Medical, dental, vision for you and dependents. Mental health budget, no receipts required.

04 · Client visits
Visits, often.

Every project team visits the client at least quarterly. Long-haul economy, good hotels, time on the ground.

05 · AI tools
Every API you need.

All AI subscriptions and APIs covered. New tools encouraged — try them, write up what you find.

06 · Family
Six months parental.

Equal for all parents. Phased return supported. Family welcome at the studio anytime.
