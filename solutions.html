09  Frequently Asked

Questions, answered.

If something isn't covered here, write to studio@xrd.technologies — we usually reply within a working day.

Does AI design the building, or do your architects?+
Our architects design. AI widens the search, audits the performance, and removes the choreography. Every line that leaves the studio has a designer's name on it.
Will my project's data train your models?+
No. Studio-private hosting is the default. Your IP, your geometry, your simulations stay in your tenant. We only learn from anonymized public datasets and our own past work.
Can you work outside Vietnam?+
Yes. Our contextual layers are strongest across Southeast Asia, but we've delivered work across the wider Asia-Pacific. We partner with local architects-of-record where needed.
Which file formats do you hand off?+
Native Rhino, Revit, IFC, and gbXML. Our Quill and Compass tools also export sheet sets to PDF and DWG with intelligent ordering.
How small is too small for an engagement?+
We've taken on competitions and pavilion-scale work alongside masterplans. The AI Co-Design retainer is designed for firms who want our help on a single intense moment — a competition, a feasibility, a façade rethink.
What does "performance" actually include?+
Operational energy, daylight quality, embodied carbon, thermal comfort, acoustics, and lifecycle cost. We can scope deeper into water, wellness, or biodiversity for projects that ask for it.
