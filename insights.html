Editorial dossier · Quarterly

Notes from the boatyards of the Mekong.

"Every joint we draw owes something to a boat builder we will never meet."

For seven months in 2025, three of us travelled the lower Mekong with cameras, calipers, and a lot of bad coffee. We measured 41 boatyards, photographed 612 lap joints, and brought home a notebook that has quietly reshaped the timber-frame catalogue we use in every project.

This dossier collects the photographs, the joinery drawings, and the eight rules of thumb we've adopted. It's available as a 64-page PDF and a hardcover at-cost from the studio.
