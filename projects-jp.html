02 · Civic Infrastructure · 2025

Mekong
Floating Pavilion.

Cần Thơ, Vietnam — a public hall that rises with the river, eight metres a year.

A buoyant civic platform tethered to four shore pylons, designed for a delta whose water table now shifts seasonally by metres. Modular HDPE pontoons, bamboo lattice roof, fully passive ventilation. Opened to the public in March.

03 · Cultural · 2026

Limestone
Music Hall.

Luang Prabang, Laos — a 600-seat hall carved into a karst cliff face.

An acoustic study of cave geometry produced a hall whose RT60 holds at 1.9s without a single panel of engineered absorption. Local stonemasons trained over eighteen months on parametric setting-out drawings; ribbon openings frame the Mekong below.
